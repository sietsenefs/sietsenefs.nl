Europe and identity

A group of students from the Radboud university asked me if i could make a book cover for their essay bundle named "Europa en identiteit". It had to visualize the identity of Europe.

- Book cover by Sietse Nefs | Radboud university

Preparation

The Radboud students had some very rough ideas about how to make the book cover with their input i designed a few sketches of the book cover and let them choose the one they liked the most. I then made that sketch into a book cover and added a few variations of that sketch so they could have a choice again. This workflow worked really good and really helped to get the students a cover exactly how they wanted it.

Result

When i was done making the book cover I send it back to the Radboud students for review and after some minor tweaks they were very pleased with the end result and used it for their essay bundle.
