NOMNOM

NOMNOM is the name of my own stylized font face made from just squares and triangles to create a childish candy brand like font wich i always wanted to make but never got around to do until now!

- render by Sietse Nefs | blender

Idea

I always loved the fonts used on candy product because they look so happy and wild. Thats why i wanted to make my own but I did want to make it unique and very stylized so after making many sketches the idea of making a font out of only two primative shapes looked like a fun challenge.

Making the font

Making the font turned out to be harder then expected. The first vew letters went really good but to make the harder letters fit in with all the others it was a real challenge and took alot of thinking and sketching but in the end i am very satisfied with the result.
