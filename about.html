 About me

Hey! I'm Sietse.

I am a Graphic Design student based in The Netherlands and in my free time i love to tinker in 3D software and with electronics. You can usually find me either tinkering, taking care of my plants or hanging out with friends (when we can go outside again).

Currently I am a last year student at Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam and do an internship at Makerpoint Rotterdam where I design a websites and make 3D models for 3D printing.
